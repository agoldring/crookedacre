Services

Mastering (Digital)

Detail-sensitive mastering for your stereo mixes. Includes instrumentals, alternate versions & revisions to ensure you are 100% satisfied. Organized deliverables in release-ready formats for labels & distributors .

Mastering (Physical)

Looking to create a physical release? I will provide additional mastered versions of your project specifically tailored for vinyl pressing, and CD replication (DDP).

Mixing

Need more than mastering? I occasionaly take on mixing or stem mastering projects. I love adding analog glue and clarity to your productions with a fresh ear. Feel free to reach out for a quote.

Studio / Process

The studio is acoustically treated to ensure accurate monitoring. I cross reference my work between Tyler Acoustics MM6x mastering speakers, Audeze LCD-X Headphones, and a mono Auratone 5C.

Conversion & monitoring path is handled by a Mytek mastering grade AD/DA. I use a carefully selected hybrid digital/analog processing chain, unique to the needs of each project.

Each project starts with a conversation about your sonic goals for the record, and how mastering can help you acheive your vision.

Deliverables provide high-resolution streaming-optimized masters, alternate versions, and any additional formats needed for distribution or archival.
