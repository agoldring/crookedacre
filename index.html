Hello!

My name is Andrew Goldring, and I’m a mastering engineer based in the mountains of Utah.

Over the past decade I've worn many hats in the music world, collaborating with artists at all levels—from DIY productions to major label releases.

For five years I lived and worked in Nashville, where I contributed to a diverse array of projects as an engineer, producer, and songwriter. Music I've worked on has ammased millions of streams, and been featured on many notable Spotify & Apple Music playlists.

I started mastering records around 2017 and began stedily sharpening my skills & building a client base. Slowly this side hustle turned into a primary focus, and I fell in love with the mindful process of finalizing records and bringing songs to their fullest potential.

Whether working with independent artists or those with established teams, I am dedicated to building meaningful relationships and delivering exceptional sonic quality in my work.

If you have any questions regarding my process, current rates, availability, or technical details, please don’t hesitate to reach out.
